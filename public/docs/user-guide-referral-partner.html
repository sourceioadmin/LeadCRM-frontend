Accepting Your Invitation

As a Referral Partner, you are invited to Leadbox by a company administrator. You will receive an invitation email at the address registered by the admin. Follow the steps below to accept the invitation and set up your account.

Step 1 — Check Your Email

Look for an invitation email from Leadbox in your inbox. The email contains a personal invitation link that is unique to you.

The invitation email you will receive in your inbox

  1. Open your email inbox and look for an email from Leadbox with the subject line containing "You're invited".
  2. Click the Accept Invitation button in the email to open the registration page.
Tip: If you don't see the email, check your Spam or Junk folder. The invitation link may expire, so accept it promptly.

Step 2 — Complete Your Registration

Clicking the invitation link opens the registration page. Fill in your details to create your Leadbox account.

The registration form — fill in your details to activate your account

FieldWhat to enterRequired?
Full NameYour full nameRequired
UsernameA unique username for your accountRequired
Phone NumberYour contact number (used for WhatsApp notifications)Required
PasswordCreate a strong passwordRequired
Confirm PasswordRe-enter your password to confirmRequired
  1. Enter your Full Name, Username, and Phone Number.
  2. Create a Password and re-enter it in the Confirm Password field.
  3. Click Accept Invitation to complete your registration.
Note: Your email address is pre-filled and cannot be changed — it was set by the administrator who invited you.
2

Logging In

Once your account is set up, you can log in to Leadbox at any time using your email and password.

The Leadbox login page — enter your email and password to sign in

  1. Go to the Leadbox login page.
  2. Enter your Email and Password.
  3. Click Sign In to access your account.
Tip: Tick Remember me if you want to stay logged in on your device.
3

Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first page you see after logging in. It gives you a quick summary of the leads you have submitted and their current status.

Your Dashboard — an overview of leads you have submitted

  1. After logging in, you will land on the Dashboard automatically.
  2. Use the sidebar on the left to navigate to other pages.
  3. The summary cards at the top show key counts — total leads, new leads, converted leads, and more.
4

Adding a Lead

As a Referral Partner, your primary role is to submit leads — people or businesses you think may be interested in your company's products or services.

The Add Lead form — fill in the lead's details and click Create Lead

  1. Click Add Lead in the left sidebar.
  2. Fill in the lead's details in the form that appears.
  3. Click Save Lead to submit it.

5

Viewing Your Leads

The My Leads page shows all the leads you have submitted. You can track each lead's status to see how far along it is in the sales process.

My Referred Leads — all leads you have submitted with their current status

  1. Click My Leads in the left sidebar.
  2. You will see a list of all leads you have added.
  3. Each lead shows the name, status, source, and when it was added.
  4. Click on any lead to view its full details.
Tip: Use the search bar at the top to quickly find a specific lead by name or company.
