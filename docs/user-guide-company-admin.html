1. Welcome to Leadbox

Leadbox is your company's all-in-one lead management tool. It helps you and your team keep track of every potential customer (lead) — from the moment they show interest right through to closing the sale.

As a Company Admin, you have full control over your company's setup. Here is a quick summary of what you can do:

Feature What It Does
Dashboard See a live summary of all your company's leads and activity
Add Lead Record a new potential customer
My Leads / All Leads Browse and manage all leads in your company
Assign Leads Hand over unassigned leads to your team members
Follow-Ups See which leads need a call or response today
Reports Understand how well your team is converting leads
Import Leads Upload a spreadsheet of leads all at once
Manage Users Invite team members and manage their roles
Settings Customise your company profile and lead categories

2. Getting Started

2.1 Creating Your Account

You only need to do this once when setting up Leadbox for your company for the first time.

  1. Open Leadbox in your web browser.
  2. Click Create Account on the login page.
  3. Fill in Step 1 – Company Details:
    • Company Name – your business name
    • Industry – pick the closest match from the list
    • Company Size – number of employees
    • Website and Phone are optional
  4. Click Next.
  5. Fill in Step 2 – Your Details:
    • Full Name, Email, Username
    • Phone Number – used for WhatsApp notifications
    • Password – must be at least 8 characters and include an uppercase letter, a number, and a special character (e.g. @, !, $)
  6. Click Create Account. You will be sent a 6-digit verification code by email.
Step 1: Enter your company details
Step 2: Enter your personal details and create a password
Your password strength is shown in real time. Aim for a "Strong" rating before submitting.

2.2 Verifying Your Email (OTP)

  1. After registration, you will be taken to the Email Verification page automatically.
  2. Check your email inbox for a message from Leadbox containing a 6-digit code.
  3. Enter the code in the boxes on screen.
  4. Click Verify. You will be logged in immediately.
Enter the 6-digit code sent to your email
Didn't receive the code? Click Resend OTP to get a new one.

2.3 Logging In

  1. Go to the Leadbox login page.
  2. Enter your Email or Username and your Password.
  3. Optional: Tick Remember me for 7 days so you don't have to log in every day.
  4. Click Sign In. You will land on your Dashboard.
The Leadbox login screen

2.4 Forgot Your Password?

  1. On the login page, click Forgot password?
  2. Enter the email address linked to your account.
  3. Click Send Reset Link. Check your email for a password reset link.
  4. Click the link in the email and enter your new password.
  5. Click Reset Password and log in with your new password.

3. Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first thing you see after logging in. It gives you an instant snapshot of your company's lead activity.

The main Dashboard — your command centre

What do the stat cards mean?

Card What it shows
Total Leads All leads added to your company so far
Unassigned Leads Leads that haven't been given to a team member yet
Today's Follow-Ups Leads that are scheduled for follow-up today
Conversion Rate Percentage of leads that became "Won" (closed deals)

Charts

  • Sales Conversion Funnel – shows how many leads are at each stage (e.g. New → Contacted → Proposal → Won). The grey bar shows leads that were Lost.
  • Leads by Source – a pie chart showing where your leads are coming from (Website, Referral, etc.).
  • Leads by Status – a bar chart breaking down leads by their current stage.

Quick Add Lead

You'll see a + Add Lead button in the top right area, or a floating circular button at the bottom right corner. Click either to quickly add a new lead without leaving the Dashboard.

Click the Refresh icon near the top right to reload the dashboard with the latest data.

4. Managing Leads

4.1 Adding a New Lead

A "lead" is a potential customer. Every time someone shows interest in your product or service, add them as a lead.

  1. Click Add Lead in the left sidebar, or use the + button on any page.
  2. Fill in the form:
    • Client Name – the contact person's name
    • Company Name – their business (if applicable)
    • Email and Phone
    • Lead Source – where this lead came from (Website, Referral, etc.)
    • Status – what stage they're at (e.g. New, Contacted)
    • Interested In – which of your products/services they're interested in
    • Expected Budget – how much they're likely to spend
    • Follow-Up Date – when you plan to contact them next
    • Notes – anything else worth remembering
  3. Click Add Lead to save.
The Add Lead form — fill in as many details as you can
Not all fields are required. You can always come back and edit the lead later to add more details.

4.2 Viewing My Leads

"My Leads" shows all the leads that are assigned to you personally.

  1. Click My Leads in the left sidebar.
  2. You will see a table of all your leads with columns like Name, Company, Status, Source, and Date Added.
  3. Use the filters at the top to narrow down by date range, source, status, urgency, or budget.
  4. Use the search bar to find a specific client by name or company.
My Leads — your personal lead list with filters

4.3 Viewing All Company Leads

"All Leads" shows every lead in your company, regardless of who they're assigned to. This is only visible to Company Admins and Managers.

  1. Click All Leads in the left sidebar.
  2. You'll see leads from all team members in one table.
  3. Use the Assigned To filter to see leads belonging to a specific team member.
All Leads — company-wide view with all team members' leads

4.4 Editing a Lead

  1. In My Leads or All Leads, find the lead you want to update.
  2. Click the Edit icon (pencil) on that row.
  3. Update any of the lead's details.
  4. Click Save to apply your changes.
You can also update a lead's Status directly from the table row — just click on the status badge and select a new one from the dropdown.

4.5 Assigning Leads to Team Members

When a lead has not been given to anyone yet (it shows as "Unassigned"), you can assign it to a specific team member.

  1. Click Assign Leads in the left sidebar.
  2. You'll see a list of all unassigned leads.
  3. Tick the checkbox next to one or more leads you want to assign.
  4. Click the Assign Selected button.
  5. Choose a team member from the dropdown.
  6. Click Assign to confirm. The leads will move off this list.
Assign unassigned leads to your team members in bulk

4.6 Bulk Actions

In All Leads, you can update or export multiple leads at once:

  1. Tick the checkbox next to each lead you want to act on. Use the checkbox in the header row to select all.
  2. A toolbar will appear at the top with options:
    • Update Status – change the status of all selected leads at once
    • Assign – assign all selected leads to a team member
    • Export – download selected leads as an Excel file
  3. Choose your action and confirm.

5. Follow-Ups

A follow-up is a reminder to call or contact a lead on a specific date. The Follow-Ups page helps you stay on top of these reminders so no lead falls through the cracks.

  1. Click Follow-Ups in the left sidebar.
  2. You'll see three groups:
    • Overdue – follow-ups that were scheduled in the past and not yet done (shown in red)
    • Today – follow-ups due today
    • Upcoming – follow-ups scheduled for future dates
  3. For each lead, you can:
    • Click Reschedule to set a new follow-up date
    • Click Update Status to mark the lead as Contacted, Won, etc.
    • Click Notes to add or edit notes for the lead
    • Click Edit Lead to open the full lead details
Follow-Ups page — overdue items are highlighted in red
Make sure to check your overdue follow-ups regularly. Leads that are left too long without contact are less likely to convert.

6. Reports

The Reports section gives you insight into how well your company is converting leads into sales. Click Reports in the left sidebar to see three types of reports.

Reports hub — choose the report you want to view

6.1 Conversion Report

This report shows you how many leads are at each stage of your sales process — like a funnel where leads enter at the top and progress toward a sale.

  1. Click Conversion Report.
  2. The chart shows each stage (e.g. New → Contacted → Proposal Sent → Won) with the number of leads at each stage.
  3. The conversion rate between stages tells you what percentage of leads are moving forward.
  4. A low conversion rate at a particular stage means that stage needs attention.
Conversion Report — see where leads are dropping off

6.2 Win vs. Loss Report

This report compares how many leads your team is winning (closing successfully) versus losing.

  1. Click Win vs. Loss Report.
  2. You'll see a breakdown by lead source and urgency level.
  3. Use this to identify which sources bring in the highest quality leads.
Win vs. Loss Report — understand what's working and what isn't

6.3 Additional Analytics

This section provides extra charts and data about your lead pipeline.

  • Leads by Source – which channels bring in the most leads
  • Leads by Status – how leads are distributed across stages
  • Leads by Urgency – breakdown by urgency level (Hot, Warm, Cold, etc.)
  • Team Performance – how many leads each team member has and their conversion rate
Additional Analytics — deeper insights into your pipeline

7. Importing Leads in Bulk

If you have a list of leads in a spreadsheet (Excel or CSV), you can upload them all at once instead of adding them one by one.

Step 1 – Download the Template

  1. Click Import Leads in the left sidebar.
  2. Click Download Template to get the correct file format.
  3. Open the template in Excel or Google Sheets and fill it in with your lead data. Do not change the column headings.

Step 2 – Upload Your File

  1. Once your file is ready, go back to the Import Leads page.
  2. Click Choose File and select your filled-in spreadsheet.
  3. Click Upload. A progress bar will show you how the import is going.
  4. When it's done, you'll see a summary: how many leads were added successfully and how many had errors.
Import Leads — upload your spreadsheet to add leads in bulk
If some rows have errors (e.g. missing required fields), those leads will be rejected. You can download the rejected rows, fix them, and re-upload.

Viewing Import History

  1. Click Import History in the left sidebar.
  2. You'll see a list of all past import jobs with their date, status, and how many leads were imported.
  3. Click on any import to download the results or the rejected rows file.
Import History — review all past bulk imports

8. Managing Your Team

As Company Admin, you can invite new users, change their roles, and deactivate accounts when needed.

  1. Click Manage Users in the left sidebar.
  2. You'll see a list of everyone in your company along with their name, email, role, and status.
Manage Users — see and control everyone in your company

Inviting a New User

  1. Click the Invite User button.
  2. Enter the person's email address and select their role (Company Manager or Team Member).
  3. Click Send Invite. They'll receive an email with a link to join your company.
Team Members and Company Managers join via the invite link. Only you (as Company Admin) can set up the company initially.

Changing a User's Role

  1. Find the user in the list and click the Edit icon (pencil).
  2. Select a new role from the dropdown.
  3. Click Save.

Deactivating a User

  1. Find the user and click the toggle switch next to their name to turn them inactive.
  2. Inactive users cannot log in, but their leads and data remain in the system.
You can reactivate an inactive user at any time by toggling them back on.

9. Settings

The Settings page lets you customise your company's profile and the categories used when managing leads. Click Settings in the left sidebar to open it.

Settings — Company tab is open by default

9.1 Company Profile

Update your company's information that appears across the app.

  1. Click the Company Settings tab.
  2. You can update:
    • Company Name, Industry, Size, Website, Phone
    • Company Logo – click the logo area to upload a new image
  3. Click Save Changes when done.

9.2 User Profile

User Profile tab — update your personal details

Update your own personal information.

  1. Click the User Profile tab.
  2. You can update your Full Name, Email, Phone Number, and Username.
  3. To change your password, click Change Password and follow the prompts.
  4. Click Save Changes.

9.3 Interested In Options

Interested In Options tab — manage your product/service categories

"Interested In" options are the products or services that your leads can be interested in. You can customise this list to match what your company offers.

  1. Click the Interested In Options tab.
  2. You'll see a list of current options.
  3. To add a new option, click + Add Option, type the name, and click Save.
  4. To edit an existing option, click the Edit icon (pencil) next to it.
  5. To delete an option, click the Delete icon (bin) and confirm.
These options appear in the "Interested In" dropdown when adding or editing a lead. Keep the list relevant to your business offerings.
